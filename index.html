Gastronomic Heritage Presentation

WELCOME REFRESHERS

Elixir Infusions

Kesar Thandai Chach

Chilled buttermilk simmered carefully with premium hand-pounded Kashmiri saffron filaments, green pistachios, and cooling garden-fresh ground mint essence.

Seasonal Gulab Sarbat

Hand-pressed heritage rose blossom water balanced cleanly with natural sweet rock candy crystals and a subtle splash of organic Key lime.

SAVORY APETIZERS

Farsan Selections

Paneer Lifafa

Crispy golden pastry envelope stuffed with spiced fresh-grated cottage cheese, native coriander leaves, green ginger juliennes, and dynamic spice blend.

Steamed Nylon Dhokla

Spongy, lightweight fermented gram flour blocks tempered meticulously with dynamic mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chilli shreds.

RAJASTHANI HERITAGE

Shahi Gravis

Rajasthani Gatta Curry

Hand-kneaded cylinder dumplings of gram flour cooked in an authentic cumin-infused, thick whipped local yogurt sauce.

Shahi Paneer Cream

Plump, hand-pressed cottage cheese cubes slow-cooked in a rich base of sweet cashew nut paste, cardamoms, and velvet cream.

NATIVE CLASSICS

Curated Sabjis

Adraki Aloo Gobhi

Copper-pan tossed fresh cauliflower florets and gold potatoes, pan-roasted dry with ginger strips, turmeric, and local spices.

Seasonal Specialty Sabji

Regional farm vegetable selected daily, cooked with lightweight cold-pressed oils to preserve original nourishment and purity.

SLOW FIRED LEGUMES

Dals & Varans

Panchmel Dal

Traditional thick wood-fired combination of five unique regional lentils, tempered with direct ghee-roasted cumin and dry red chillies.

Maharashtrian Varan

Smooth golden lentils slow-boiled with organic turmeric, curry leaves, ginger, and a strong pinch of dynamic premium asafoetida (hing).

CLAY OVEN & HEARTH

Warm Breads

Hot Ghee Phulkas & Rotlas

Hand-rolled wheat breads served steaming hot straight onto your royal plate, brushed generously with hand-churned A2 cow ghee.

Papad & House Relishes

Crispy hand-stretched lentil papads served along with our curated family pickles, green mustard relishes, and mint pestos.

CONFECTIONERY LEGACIES

Chilled Elaneer Payasam

Velvety blend of fresh tender coconut shavings, condensed cardamom-scented milk, saffron threads, and golden raisins.
